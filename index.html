How to Delete Your Account in Goals App

We value your privacy and provide you with the option to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the Goals App on your device.
  2. Navigate to the More section at the bottom of the screen (icon with three dots or the word "More").
  3. Scroll down and select Delete Account from the menu options.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your decision to delete your account.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

Your personal information, including your account data, preferences, and usage history, will be permanently deleted from our servers.

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days to comply with legal obligations or for dispute resolution purposes.
